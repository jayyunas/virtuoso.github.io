Services

Our list of awesome services.

Students are often unable to pursue their dream colleges abroad simply because they did not have access to enough information about the application process and how to approach it. It is difficult to find private college counselors who give you tailored advice, with fees going up to $200/hour (INR 14,000). We provide this and more - completely free of charge.

Fully responsive

Our students will get back to any quick inquiries or requests to chat live within 48 hours.

Essay Feedback

Our students will provide objective evaluation of your work, helping you improve your essays both stylistically and content wise.

Narrowing Your List

After comparing different colleges and the opportunities that they have to offer, we will guide you in shortlisting colleges that best fit your interests and personality.

Finding and Comparing Colleges

It is easy to be baffled by which factors to focus primarily on when looking at the thousands of college opportunities available abroad. Does the college offer a major or club that really intrigues you? Do they have research opportunities that will help you gain an edge in the career of your choice? Does the faculty to student ratio suit your learning style? Hear how our students thought about these factors and seek their guidance in comparing colleges.

Insight into College Life and Culture Abroad

Discuss your concerns about studying abroad in a country whose culture does not align with yours, and hear about how our students dealt with these differences.

Understanding the Application Timeline

It can be immensely beneficial to start thinking about college as early as freshman year (grade 9) since admissions abroad are based upon a holistic review of the applicant —not their grades and scores alone.
